Fiddlesticks- The ancient fear

Abilities

Scarecrow Effigy

Fiddlesticks can place an effigy as a ward to reveal enemy locations

Enemy champion approaching an effigy will activate it, causing the effigy to fake a random action, after which the effigy will fall apart.

Terrify

Passive: If Fiddlesticks spells damage an enemy while unseen while Fiddle is outside of combat, Fiddlesticks terrifies the enemy

Fiddlesticks terrifies an enemy, causing them to flee. If the passive effect of this spell has been recently triggered, Terrify will instead amplify damage dealt by Fiddlesticks to the enemy

Bountiful Harvest

Fiddlesticks drains the souls of all enemies around itself,dealing damage over 2s, with the final tick dealing a portion of their missing health as magic damage

Reap

Fiddlesticks unleashes dark magic from it's Scythe, dealing magic damage and slowing enemies hit for 1.5s in a crescent shaped area at a target position.

Crowstorm

Fiddlesticks channels then blinks to the target point- unleashing a murder of crows that flock wildly around it- dealing magic damage each second to all enemy units in the area.
